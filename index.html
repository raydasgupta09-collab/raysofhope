Hi, I'm Ray - Student Artist & Fundraiser

I'm a junior at Scarsdale High School, and art has always been my passion. When my aunt was diagnosed with cancer last year, it deeply affected me. However, I was able to see firsthand how much cancer hospitals do for patients and families. I wanted to find a way to give back, so I'm selling my artwork to raise money for cancer research and patient support programs.

Why This Matters

Cancer affects millions of families every year. Local cancer hospitals provide not just treatment, but hope, support, and care during the most difficult times. Every dollar raised through this initiative goes directly to support patient care programs, research, and family support services. Together, we can make a real difference in the lives of cancer patients and their families.
